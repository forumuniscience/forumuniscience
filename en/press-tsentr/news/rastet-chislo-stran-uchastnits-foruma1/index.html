Co-Chairman of the Organizing Committee, MSUMD Rector, Professor Yanushevich Oleg

Dear colleagues,

The aim of the 1st international forum of university science is to study the contribution of fundamental research into inflammatory disease management. The key topic runs as ‘Fundamental Research and Translational Medicine for Systemic and Autoimmune Inflammations Control’. The plenary meeting will be devoted to progress in predictive and preventive areas of translational medicine. Planned are sessions in immunology, conferences on burning problems of diagnosis fundamentals and other major issues. An international conference on biomedical rheumatology (BRIC) initiated by Prof. Kusuki Nishioka, Director of Institute for Biomedical Research of Tokyo University, will also make part of the event.
