how to get there by car

Sheremetyevo International Airport - SVO
Hotel direction: 18.8 mile(s) SW. From Sheremetievo International Airport proceed along Mezhdunarodnoe shosse, take the ramp onto Leningradskoe shosse, continue onto Leningradskiy prospect up to the 3rd ring road. Turn to Sushevsky Val and proceed till the exit toward Verzemneka street, continue onto Shepkina street. Turn right onto Samarskaya street. AZIMUT Moscow Olympic Hotel is on the right side. Please call the hotel to arrange for transportation.



Moscow Domodedovo Airport - DME
Hotel direction: 31 mile(s) NW. From Domodedovo International Airport proceed along Kashirskoe shosse, continue onto Varshavskoe shosse that becomes the Bolshaya Tulskaya street, turn right to the Danilovsky Val and slight left to the Dubininskaya street. Turn right onto Sadovoe ring, turn right to Shepkina street. Turn left onto Samarskaya street. AZIMUT Moscow Olympic Hotel is on the right side. Please call the hotel to arrange for transportation.



Vnukovo Airport - VKO

Hotel direction: 21.1 mile(s) NW From Vnukovo Airport proceed along Kievskoe shosse that becomes the Leninsky prospect, turn left towards Sadovoe Ring and proceed till exit to Tsvetnoy Boulevard street. Turn left onto Olimpiyskiy prospect. Then turn right to the Samarskaya street. AZIMUT Moscow Olympic Hotel is on the left side. Please call the hotel to arrange for transportation.

how to get there on foot

Metro Station - Prospekt Mira (radial) of the subway exit on the left, go to first traffic light and turn left into the alley. Next, go straight, the hotel will be on the right side.
